Associate Professor

Cecilia Ovesdotter Alm, Ph.D.

Cecilia's research interests focus on linguistic sensing and language-inclusive sensing with text, speech, and multimodal data. She has special interests in meaning, affect, multimodality, health/wellbeing, and education, and is dedicated to supporting student research and training experiences. Cecilia is the PI of an NSF award entitled REU Site: Computational Sensing. She is often involved in interdisciplinary research collaborations. Besides her Ph.D. in Linguistics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (centered on computational linguistics), she also holds a Certificate of Advanced Study in Language and Speech Processing and a Certificate of Graduate Specialization in Computational Science and Engineering from UIUC.
